Deep Dive · Department 03

How the Records team works without people.

Provider Identifier
Extracts providers from intake, bills, prior records; dedupes against the graph.
Records Requester
Generates HIPAA + provider-specific form; sends via fax / portal / e-fax MCP.
Records Chaser
Daily age check; escalates at T-14, T-21, T-30.
Bill Collector
Pulls bills from records or sends bill-specific requests; extracts CPT / UB-04 lines.
ICD-10 / CPT Mapper
Codes diagnoses and treatments; links straight to the specials calculator.
Treatment-Gap Detector
Flags missed PT, MMI date estimate, treatment-trajectory anomalies.
Worked Example
Mendez v. Allstate · Monday morning
8:14:00amSt. Joseph ER bill arrives in scanned mail (DOS 2026-05-12, $4,217.40).
8:14:03amVault classifies as medical_bill.
8:14:08amProvider Identifier resolves St. Joseph Hospital.
8:14:12amBill Collector extracts CPT 99284 + 72148 ($4,217.40).
8:14:18amICD-10 Mapper codes S13.4XXA (cervical sprain) + S06.0X0A (concussion).
8:14:22amGap Detector flags missed PT at Foothill Physical Therapy.
8:14:25amDrafts SMS to Maria: "Hi Maria, we noticed you missed your 5/22 PT — should we reschedule?"
8:14:31amAttorney approves SMS in 6 seconds.
23 seconds of agent work. 6 seconds of human work. Zero paralegal hours.

The Trust Model

Agents know when to stop.

Every action carries a confidence score. The gate decides who acts — the agent, the human, or both.

Auto
≥ 0.85
Agent acts alone
Example
"Records request to a provider Mendez previously listed in intake. Sends without asking."
HITL
0.50 – 0.85
Human reviews
Example
"Counter-offer to Sarah Kim. Attorney reviews 30 seconds, taps approve."
Drop
< 0.50
Escalate & explain
Example
"Ambiguous demand-letter inbound — escalates to senior partner with reasoning."

Same gating proven in Aedis healthcare for 14 months.

The Close
