Engineering

Built like a bank. Not like a SaaS demo.

PHI doesn’t leave your VPC. Agents don’t hold credentials. Every action is hash-chained before it executes. These are defaults — not features you turn on after the audit finds something.

Credential isolation

Agents never hold keys.

Credentials live in AWS Secrets Manager, flow through Anthropic Vault, and are injected into agent calls through a hardened MCP proxy at request time. Rotation is automated on a 90-day cycle for every critical credential.

  • Zero credentials in agent memory
  • Per-system, per-action OAuth scopes
  • Revocation in under 15 minutes, fleet-wide
Immutable audit

Every decision is replayable.

Each action is hash-chained with SHA-256, ordered by monotonic sequence, and exportable for auditors. A 7-level governance engine routes from observe-only up to CFO and GC sign-off based on the dollar amount, clinical risk, and regulatory sensitivity of the decision.

  • SOX & HIPAA-compatible audit format
  • Per-agent kill-switch with reason capture
  • Decision replay with input snapshot
In-tenant PHI

PHI never leaves your VPC.

The platform runs on AWS Bedrock inside your VPC, covered by your existing BAA, with zero data retention configured at the model layer. Row-level security on every table. JWKS rotation for signing keys. JWT-authenticated WebSocket for the live decision queue.

  • No PHI to external model providers
  • RLS enforced at the Postgres layer
  • Anomaly-detected kill-switch per agent

A day on Aedis

Four phases. One queue. The operating model your team already wants.

Overnight

Agents read every system.

Clinical, finance, workforce, regulatory feeds. By 6 a.m. there’s a synthesized executive briefing waiting for the CEO, COO, and each Regional VP — ranked by what changed.

6:30 a.m.

Executives open one queue.

The morning starts on a single screen. Decisions ranked by blast radius. Each card is self-contained: agent recommendation, full evidence, citations, three forward-looking options.

Through the day

Humans approve. Agents execute. Everything logs.

Operators tap approve, deny, or modify. Each click is hash-chained with the agent’s reasoning. Anomalies route to the queue in seconds — never missed in a Friday inbox.

Continuously

Posture improves with every cycle.

The knowledge graph learns from every approved decision. Patterns emerge across facilities. The fleet gets sharper — without any vendor patch cycle, ever.
